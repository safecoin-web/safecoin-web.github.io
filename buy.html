Buying Safecoins for bitcoins (XBT)

Bitcoin address to be annouced.

How to buy Safecoin with bitcoins

  1. Backers will receive 17,000 MaidSafeCoins per bitcoin
  2. Send the amount of bitcoins from your wallet to the SAFE exodus address for Bitcoin, published above. Please be sure to send only bitcoins to the Bitcoin address, or the transactions will be considered invalid. If your funds are returned to you it is either because you have sent currency to the wrong exodus address (.i.e. mastercoin to the bitcoin address), or all coins have been sold and the sale has ended.
  3. Your purchase will be recorded directly on the Bitcoin block chain.
  4. As the funds are received MaidSafeCoins will be issued and sent to the address used to send the funds. Please note that it may take up to 24 hours for these transactions to be processed.
  5. Mastercoin block explorers (such as masterchest.info) can be used to view your MaidSafeCoin balance. A Mastercoin wallet (downloadable or web-based) is needed to send MaidSafeCoins to other addresses. Mastercoin wallets are available at: mastercoinwallets.org.
  6. Using your Mastercoin wallet, it will be possible to send MaidSafeCoins to the MasterXchange to trade MaidSafeCoins masterxchange.com
  7. Once the full SAFE network is launched, MaidSafeCoins will be swapped for safecoins at a 1:1 ratio.

Attention please use a bitcoin wallet such as Blockchain.info with which you control the private key to your public address.
